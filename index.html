Civic Engagement has never been this easy.

At VoteBetter our mission is to help students and student representatives alike navigate the landscape of student campus politics, form a collective voice on the issues they care about, and effectively organize for advocacy and change.

Staying Informed Made Easy

Brush up on your knowledge of campus politics by accessing important information about the structure, processes, and role of your student organization right in the palm of your hand.

Making constitutions and bylaws easy to understand

Content from contributors who have previous experience in office

Meet Your Candidates

Say goodbye to getting buried in information between the website of your student organization, slate websites, and endless facebook pages and twitter feeds. VoteBetter features all individuals running for office on one platform so you can finally accomplish what you set out for- getting to know your candidates.

For Candidates:

Levels the playing field for independent (non-slate-affiliated) candidates

Saves money for individuals looking to purchase their own domain so funds can be invested elsewhere

Relinquishes the burden of having to design your own website from scratch (p.s. you can still add a link to your site once it’s complete)

Have Your Say

Ask for more from your representatives with a built-in tool for expressing your ideas. Whether you have a concern about transportation or a proposal for a new student service, bring your thoughts to the forefront of political debate and foster dialogue around issues that are important to you.

For Candidates:

Stay on top of hot topic issues that matter most to your peers

Convenient way to test your assumptions of student needs and concerns

Engage with constituents through an in-app commenting feature

Compare Candidates

VoteBetter respects your time. That’s why we’ve streamlined the process of comparing competing candidates. Active candidates will be prompted to respond to the top three up-voted hot topics for their respective position, empowering you to make the decision on who earns your vote.

For Candidates:

Keep track of what your political counterparts are offering the student body

Receive a notification when your competitor makes changes to their platform

Easily update your profile to address hot topics and concerns

Track Campaign Promises

Once the elections are over the real work begins. Stay on top of the execution of campaign promises with polimeters specifically designed for each elected executive representative. Polimeters are updated daily and connected to social media and local news networks you know and trust.

For Candidates:

Like you, we know that transparency and accountability are more than just buzzwords. Keep your constituents in-the-know with a tried and true tool for demonstrating political integrity.

Better Together

Our FAQ page features a peer-to-peer forum which contains additional content one may need to enhance their experience of civic engagement on campus. If there is a question that the community can’t answer, we will do the research for you or send an inquiry to the student organization on your behalf and update the page as soon as we hear back.

Spreading awareness on topics such as:

Different avenues through which you can keep your elected representatives in check

The feasibility of promises made on the campaign trail (i.e. what candidates do and do not have the power to accomplish)

Initiatives your student organization has accomplished in the past

Integrated Social Media Capabilities

Respond to your @s, comments, and replies all in one place. VoteBetter also makes it easy to follow outspoken students on campus who are advocating for issues that are important to you and receive a notification when they make a post.

For Candidates:

This feature that was specifically designed with you in mind, because leadership is more than a title and advocacy doesn’t just happen when you are in office.

Meet other thought leaders on campus

Build your team identity and brand in an easy and purposeful way

Get to know who your competition might be when election season rolls around

FAQs

Who can use VoteBetter?

Student organizations from different postsecondary schools can benefit from using VoteBetter. The platform can be used for civic engagement within different levels of student government (e.g. program/ course unions as well as the student organization on campus at large). During sign-up, users will be asked to indicate the campus they are affiliated with so that they can be connected to the corresponding online community.

Is VoteBetter ready for download?

No. VoteBetter is currently in the early stages of development. The purpose of this landing page is to share the concept and proposed features of VoteBetter with postsecondary students and gauge public interest in the platform. If you haven't already, please let us know what you think!

Who can use VoteBetter?

From the get-go, the development of VoteBetter has been informed by the experiences of students. We take pride in being aggressively driven by the needs and interests of the postsecondary community and are always looking for additional team members. Drop us a line at votebetterapp@gmail.com to learn more about opportunities to work with us.
