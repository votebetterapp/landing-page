Meet Your Candidates

Say goodbye to getting buried in information between the website of your student organization, slate websites, and endless facebook pages and twitter feeds. VoteBetter features all individuals running for office on one platform so you can finally accomplish what you set out for- getting to know your candidates.

For Candidates:

Levels the playing field for independent (non-slate-affiliated) candidates

Saves money for individuals looking to purchase their own domain so funds can be invested elsewhere

Relinquishes the burden of having to design your own website from scratch (p.s. you can still add a link to your site once it’s complete)

Have Your Say

Ask for more from your representatives with a built-in tool for expressing your ideas. Whether you have a concern about transportation or a proposal for a new student service, bring your thoughts to the forefront of political debate and foster dialogue around issues that are important to you.

For Candidates:

Stay on top of hot topic issues that matter most to your peers

Convenient way to test your assumptions of student needs and concerns

Engage with constituents through an in-app commenting feature

Compare Candidates

VoteBetter respects your time. That’s why we’ve streamlined the process of comparing competing candidates. Active candidates will be prompted to respond to the top three up-voted hot topics for their respective position, empowering you to make the decision on who earns your vote.

For Candidates:

Keep track of what your political counterparts are offering the student body

Receive a notification when your competitor makes changes to their platform

Easily update your profile to address hot topics and concerns
